# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** August 28, 2025 **Last Updated:** November 13, 2025

## Introduction

This privacy policy explains how the Yuu-Chan Discord bot collects, uses, and protects your information when you use its services.

## Information Collection

The Bot may collect and store the following types of information for functionality and moderation:

- Discord User IDs, Server IDs, Channel IDs (for features, moderation, and configuration).
- Messages or commands sent to the Bot (for processing, automod, and responding).
- Server configuration data (e.g., welcome channels, log channels, birthday channels, automod settings).
- Moderation records (warnings, kicks, bans).
- XP, points, and level data for users (for leveling and rewards).
- Role assignment and ownership data (for auto roles, buyable roles, reaction roles).
- Birthday dates (MM-DD format) for birthday wishes.
- Reaction role mappings and drop event participation.
- Blacklisted words and links for automod and link protection.

The Bot does **not** collect or store personal information such as email addresses, passwords, payment information, or message content outside of moderation/logging features.

## Use of Information

Collected information is used solely to provide and improve the Bot's features, including:

- Responding to commands and automating server tasks.
- Managing server configurations (welcome messages, logs, birthday wishes, automod).
- Moderation actions (warnings, kicks, bans, purges).
- XP, leveling, and points systems.
- Role management (auto roles, buyable roles, reaction roles).
- Random drops and reward distribution.
- Ensuring compliance with server rules and automod protection.

## Data Storage

- Data is stored securely in server-side files and is only accessible to the Bot and its owner.
- No data is shared with third parties.
- Data is retained only as long as necessary for Bot functionality.

## User Rights

- You may request deletion of your data (XP, points, birthdays, warnings, etc.) by contacting the Bot owner.
- Server administrators may clear configuration data using Bot commands.

## Security

- The Bot uses reasonable measures to protect stored data from unauthorized access.
- Only the Bot owner has access to stored data files.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted in the bot's documentation or support server.

## Contact

For questions or requests regarding your data, please contact the Bot owner: @ennariaa on Discord.
