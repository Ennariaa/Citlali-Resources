# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** August 28, 2025

## Introduction

This privacy policy explains how the Yuu-Chan Discord bot collects, uses, and protects your information when you use its services.

## Information Collection

The Bot may collect the following types of information:
- User IDs, server IDs, and channel IDs for functionality and moderation.
- Messages or commands sent to the Bot (for processing and responding).
- Configuration data such as welcome channels, log channels, and warning records.

The Bot does **not** collect or store personal information such as email addresses, passwords, or payment information.

## Use of Information

Collected information is used solely to provide and improve the Bot's features, such as:
- Responding to commands and automating server tasks.
- Managing server configurations (e.g., welcome messages, logs, warnings).
- Ensuring moderation and compliance with server rules.

## Data Storage

- Data is stored securely and is only accessible to the Bot and its owner.
- No data is shared with third parties.

## User Rights

- You may request the deletion of your data by contacting the Bot owner.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted in the bot's documentation or support server.

## Contact

For questions or requests regarding your data, please contact the Bot owner. @ennariaa on Discord.
